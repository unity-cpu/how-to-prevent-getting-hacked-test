How Hacks Happen

How Hacks Happen

Hacks often begin with phishing emails, weak passwords, or outdated software that attackers exploit. Social engineering techniques trick users into giving away access without realizing it.

Protect Yourself

How to Protect Yourself

  • Use strong, unique passwords with a password manager
